🧑‍💻 Self-serve fixes for the top 10 issues

1. "I signed in but I don't see my family"

Your family members each need their own Orbito account and must be added to your Circle. Head to Profile → Circle → Add Member and share the invite code.

2. "My location shows the wrong spot on the map"

Orbito uses your phone's GPS via expo-location. If your position is off: - Make sure Location Services are ON in your phone settings - Make sure Orbito has "While Using the App" permission (not "Never") - Restart the app once to re-anchor the map - Check that you're not on airplane mode

3. "The map is empty / doesn't move"

  • Orbito's map only shows people in your Circle or Constellation. Add someone.
  • On web, you must grant your browser's location prompt (top-left / bottom-left of your browser)
  • Try Ghost Mode off (Profile → Privacy) — if Ghost Mode is on, YOU won't broadcast

4. "Ghost Mode isn't hiding me"

Ghost Mode freezes your position and shows "Going ghost 👻" to your Circle. It does NOT delete you from the map — that's intentional, so your Circle knows you're safe but opted out. If you want to leave entirely, remove yourself from the Circle.

5. "How do I redeem a referral code?"

Profile → Invites card → "Have a code?" button → paste it. Only works in your first 14 days on Orbito.

6. "My trust streak broke — how do I restart it?"

Ping the person you had a streak with. Both of you being active + non-ghost for a day starts a fresh streak at day 1. Streaks can't be manually restored — that's the point.

7. "I paid but my plan says Free"

Sync issue. Try: - Force-close and reopen the app - Profile tab should show your plan within 60 seconds of payment - If still stuck 5 minutes later — email us your Stripe receipt and we'll fix it

8. "How do I cancel my subscription?"

Auto-renewal is OFF by default at Orbito — your subscription won't auto-renew. When your period ends, you're back on Free automatically. No cancellation needed.

If you bought through the App Store or Play Store, cancel there too (Apple ID → Subscriptions / Google Play → Payments & subscriptions).

9. "I want a refund"

Within 14 days of purchase, we'll refund you no questions asked. Email support@orbito.life with your account email and the approximate purchase date. We usually process within 3-5 business days.

App Store / Play Store purchases follow Apple / Google refund policies — we can't override those, but we'll help you navigate them.

10. "Crash detection is triggering when I'm not crashing"

Sorry! Adjust sensitivity in Profile → Safety → Crash detection sensitivity (Low / Medium / High). Motion-sensor false positives happen if you drop your phone, ride a rollercoaster, etc. In doubt — cancel the 30s countdown when it appears.

🗑 Delete your account

Both Apple and Google require in-app account deletion. To fully delete your Orbito account:

  1. Open Orbito
  2. Tap Profile (bottom right)
  3. Scroll to bottom → Delete Account
  4. Confirm with your Google Sign-In
  5. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

What gets deleted: - Your account, name, email, avatar - Your position and location history - Your Circle & Constellation memberships - Your Ledger entries (both yours and mentions of you elsewhere are anonymized to "a former user") - Your subscription (cancelled + non-refundable unless you're within the 14-day window) - Your referral history

What we keep (legal / audit): - Anonymized transaction records for 7 years (tax law) - Anonymized aggregate stats (not linked to your identity)

Alternatively, email privacy@orbito.life with "Delete my account" and your account email — we'll process manually.

📤 Export your data

You can request a copy of everything Orbito has stored about you. Email privacy@orbito.life with subject "Data export request" and your account email. We'll send you a JSON archive within 30 days.
